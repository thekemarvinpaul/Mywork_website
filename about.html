About Lecrie

Interior Architecture • Landscape Design • Luxury Living

Based in Kampala, Lecrie is a luxury interior and landscape design studio committed to creating refined, timeless spaces.

We merge architecture, aesthetics, and functionality to deliver environments that elevate everyday living. From private residences to commercial developments, we collaborate with homeowners and developers to transform spaces into art.

Every project begins with listening — understanding your lifestyle, your aspirations, and your aesthetic sensibility before a single line is drawn.
